Kim Kardashian and Kanye West are taking the rest of 2016 off and we get it

Brittany MaloolyDecember 5, 2016

Kim Kardashian and Kanye West have had an emotional year. First, Kim is robbed of some of her most prized possessions as well as her sense of safety in Paris, and, then, Kanye is hospitalized right before Thanksgiving for exhaustion. Now that Kanye is at home recovering with his family, it should come as no surprise that the pair is “taking the rest of 2016 off,” according to Page Six.

What this means exactly is unclear. Kim has been on a hiatus from her social media profiles since the scary robbery, so it’s likely that silence will continue. Kanye has already cancelled his tour. Page Six says, “[W]e hear they’re planning to call off the rest of their engagements until 2017 to spend time with kids, North and Saint.”
