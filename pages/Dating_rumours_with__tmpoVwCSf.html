Dating rumours with Maneesh Sharma have died down now: Parineeti Chopra

bollywood Updated: Dec 05, 2016 20:07 IST
ANI

Parineeti Chopra spoke about her relationship rumour with her director Maneesh Sharma on the latest episode of Koffee With Karan. (PTI)

It was indeed a no filter Koffee with Karan Johar and his guests Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Roy Kapur, in the fifth season’s fifth episode.

The ‘dear buddies’ and Daawat-E-Ishq co-stars were out there, speaking about their life, work and relationship decisively.

One such was when the 28-year-old spoke about her relationship rumour with her director Maneesh Sharma.

Parineeti Chopra and Aditya Roy Kapoor on Koffee With Karan. (Star World)

When KJo asked Pari to ‘tell the tale’ on the ‘conjecture’ about her Ladies vs Ricky Bahl and Shuddh Desi Romance director Maneesh Sharma, with whom her relationship rumour went on for years, she said, “The rumours of us dating have always been there.”

She added, “But I think they have died down now. Now the rumours say, she has broken up and is depressed and what not.”

When asked if she is really so, Parineeti replied, “No, I am not.”

Further during the discussion, the Kill Dil star made it clear that even after working with the ‘most-wanted mundas’ of B-Town- Aditya, Varun Dhawan and Sidharth Malhotra, there were no rumours with them because she does not want to date an actor.

“I have been friend-zoned by everybody. It’s good. I don’t want to date an actor. I want to date a non-actor,” she firmly stated.

When Karan countered, “You want to date a director?” she replied, “I don’t know but definitely not an actor.”
